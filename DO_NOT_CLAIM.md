# Do Not Claim Ledger

GHP does not currently claim:

- completed ToE,
- proof of GR/QFT/Standard Model recovery,
- proof of consciousness,
- proof of dark matter,
- proof of simulation theory,
- proof from Tao,
- proof from software,
- proof from toy models,
- proof from CERN / DESI / Nobel / Bell,
- proof from VPH,
- proof from Ricci,
- proof from ternary operator.

## Safe Language

Use:

- `motivates`
- `suggests`
- `candidate`
- `toy telemetry`
- `bridge map`
- `open conjecture`

Avoid:

- `proves`
- `solves`
- `confirms`
- `derives`

unless the claim is theorem-grade or explicitly verified inside the stated domain.
