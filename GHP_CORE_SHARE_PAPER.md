The strongest current core is categorical. Fibonacci has total quantum dimension

\[

The stronger physical claim remains open: nature may select observer-boundary architectures by an extremal or balance principle whose lowest admissible non-abelian, self-referential, computationally universal closure is Fibonacci. The next serious task is not symbolic expansion but formal construction: the observer-boundary must become a mathematical object with a write law, finite-access projection, recoverability structure, and falsifiable bridge to ordinary physics.

## 5. Mathematical Spine

Let \(D^2 = \sum_i d_i^2\) denote total quantum dimension.

**Minimality proposition.** In the class of unitary modular categories with at least one non-invertible simple object and braiding-universal computational power, Fibonacci is the minimum-cost architecture by total quantum dimension, with

\[
D^2 = 1+\phi^2 = 2+\phi.
\]

Fibonacci has two simple objects, \(1\) and \(\tau\), with fusion rule

\[
\tau \otimes \tau = 1 \oplus \tau.
\]

This gives

\[
d_\tau^2 = 1+d_\tau,
\]

so \(d_\tau=\phi\). The golden ratio enters as an algebraic consequence of the fusion rule, not as a numerical fit.
