# Ricci-Fibonacci Toy Report

The Ricci-Fibonacci toy tested candidate soft-saturation behavior of the form

`R(k) = k^m (C-k)^n`

with Fibonacci-weighted exponents.

## Summary

Interior Fibonacci-like equilibria appeared in toy runs, and Ricci-style variants avoided hard saturation better than simple write-heavy baselines. Perturbation recovery remained mixed and did not justify promotion beyond toy status.

## Status

Toy telemetry only. Not proof. Not physics evidence.
